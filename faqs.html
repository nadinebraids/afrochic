2. How long does a typical braiding session take?

The duration of a braiding session varies depending on the style. On average, a session can last between 2 to 6 hours.

5. Do I need to bring my own hair for braiding, or do you provide it?

We provide the hair for most of our braiding services, but if you prefer to bring your own, that is also welcome.

Yes, we are experienced with all hair types and textures, and we tailor our techniques to best suit your hair.
