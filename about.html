About Nadine

My name is Nadine, and I am a wife, mother, and professional hair braider with over 10 years of experience. I have worked in Florida, New Mexico, and now operate from Midtown, Atlanta. Hair braiding is not just a service for me, it’s an art, and my clients’ satisfaction is my top priority.

I offer personalized African braiding styles tailored to your preferences, and my goal is to make every client feel beautiful and confident. I believe in the power of creativity and dedication, which is reflected in every braid I create. Your beauty deserves the best care, and I am here to provide it.
